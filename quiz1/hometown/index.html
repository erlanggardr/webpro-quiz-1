About Jember

A brief description of my hometown

Jember is a vibrant city located in East Java, Indonesia. It is famous for its unique culture called Pandalungan, a harmonious blend of Javanese and Madurese traditions. The people of Jember are known for their warm hospitality and are mostly engaged in agriculture, plantations, and fishing. The city is also well-known for its natural beauty, coffee and tobacco plantations, and the colorful annual Jember Fashion Carnaval, which attracts visitors from all over the world.
