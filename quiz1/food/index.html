Local Food from Jember

Some local picks

Tape

This isn't radio tape or duct tape. It's tape, a food made by fermenting cassava using yeast, which converts starch into sugar, resulting in a sweet and slightly sour taste and a softer, more watery texture.

It's also my favorite food.

Pecel gudeg

Pecel gudeg can be found at every food stall in Jember Regency. Pecel gudeg is a combination of pecel and gudeg, consisting of white rice with chopped long beans, drizzled with pecel sauce, and topped with young jackfruit gudeg, beef with age spices, fried tofu chili sauce, pecel, peyek (rice crackers), and a slice of fried tempeh.

Wedang Cor

Wedang cor is perfect for Jember's weather, as it is an area with high rainfall. This evening drink is made from a mixture of sweetened condensed milk, sticky rice tape, and ginger tea. While simple, this drink is incredibly delicious and unique, with the distinctive sweetness of sticky rice tape and milk combined with the warmth of ginger.
